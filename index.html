About

I am a 2nd-year Ph.D. student in the Human-Computer Interaction Lab at Santa Clara University, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Kai Lukoff. My research study how people engage with digital technologies, with a particular focus on digital well-being. I am especially interested in designing tools that help people manage distraction and use technology more intentionally.

Before coming to Santa Clara, I interned at several tech companies across the Bay Area as a Product Designer. I hold a Master's degree in Information Science from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Indiana University Bloomington.
