About

I am a 2nd-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Santa Clara University, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Kai Lukoff. My research lies at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Artificial Intelligence. Specifically, I focus on designing and building autonomous agents and highly personalized AI assistants that seamlessly integrate into complex daily workflows.

Before coming to Santa Clara, I interned at several tech companies across the Bay Area as a Product Designer. I hold a Master's degree in Information Science from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Indiana University.
